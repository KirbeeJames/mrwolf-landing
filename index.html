Meet Mr. Wolf — your child's favourite time-teaching companion

The fun, colourful app for children to master telling the time through interactive timers, real clock faces, exciting games and quizzes.

🎯 Designed for children aged 4–10

Everything they need to learn the time

Six tools in one app — no ads, no subscriptions, no data collection.

⏱️

Colourful Timers

Visual countdown timers with colour-coded segments that make time tangible. Children see and feel time passing as the colours sweep around the clock face.

🕐

Real Clock Faces

Interactive analogue and digital clocks that children can manipulate. Drag the hands, read the time, and switch between 12-hour and 24-hour formats.

🎮

5 Fun Games

From matching clock times to racing against Mr. Wolf, five different game modes keep children engaged while building real time-telling confidence.

❓

Quiz Mode

Test their knowledge with progressive quizzes that adapt from simple o'clock times through quarter hours to precise five-minute intervals.

🌙

Day & Night Mode

A gentle night mode with darker colours for bedtime routines, plus a day mode that teaches the concept of AM and PM naturally.

🔒

100% Child-Safe

Zero data collection, zero ads, zero in-app purchases, zero external links. Works completely offline. Designed with COPPA and GDPR-K compliance.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

🔒 Short version: We collect zero personal data. The app works fully offline and never communicates with any server.

Who We Are

Mr. Wolf Timer & Clock Teacher ("the App") is developed and published by Gain Automations. If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at privacy@gainautomations.com.

Information We Collect

We collect no personal information whatsoever. The App does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email address, location, or any other personal details.

The App stores only anonymous, device-local preferences (such as your chosen language and colour theme). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K)

Mr. Wolf Timer & Clock Teacher is designed for children aged 4–10. We take children's privacy extremely seriously and fully comply with COPPA and the GDPR's provisions for children's data.

  • We do not collect any personal data from children or adults
  • We do not display advertising of any kind
  • We do not include social features, chat, or external links
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties

Internet Access

The App works completely offline. No internet connection is required or used at runtime. The only network activity occurs during the initial download from the App Store.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, analytics platforms, or social media services.

Data Storage

All data (language preference, theme selection) is stored locally on your device using the operating system's standard local storage mechanisms. Uninstalling the App removes all such data.

In-App Purchases

There are no in-app purchases. The App is provided as a single, complete purchase with all features included.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this Privacy Policy, the new version will be posted at this URL and the "Last updated" date above will be changed.

Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please email us at privacy@gainautomations.com.
